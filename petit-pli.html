In their first two years on earth, children grow seven sizes. Thats a lot of clothes. Petit Pli reduces waste, by colliding fashion with aerospace engineering, to create innovative garments that grow with your child.

This inspired an identity influenced by the expandable technology. Along with a fun e-commerce packaging solution, which extends the life of the FSC sourced cardboard; transforming a functional box into an object for play. A solution which reduced the packaging cost per unit by 81.5%.

Since launch, the brand has gained recognition from Sir Richard Branson whilst introducing new lines in infant-wear, adult-wear and protective face masks.
